How to Add Custom Areas

Custom areas are added inside the .app-content div, before or between your regular card content. No special classes are needed — just add your HTML:

<main class="app-main">
  <!-- Content Header (breadcrumb) -->
  \{{> content/content-header pageTitle="Page Title"}}

  <div class="app-content">
    <div class="w-full px-4">

      <!-- Custom Banner Area -->
      <div class="rounded-lg bg-gradient-to-r from-blue-600 to-indigo-700
                  text-white p-6 mb-4">
        <h3>Welcome Banner</h3>
        <p>Custom content here...</p>
      </div>

      <!-- Custom Stats Row -->
      <div class="grid grid-cols-4 gap-3 mb-4">
        <div class="bg-white rounded-lg p-4">...</div>
      </div>

      <!-- Regular Card Content -->
      <div class="card">...</div>

    </div>
  </div>
</main>
Common Custom Area Patterns:
  • Welcome banner — Gradient hero with user greeting and CTAs
  • Quick stats row — Compact metric cards between breadcrumb and content
  • Secondary toolbar — Action buttons and filters below the main header
  • Alert/announcement bar — Dismissible system notifications
  • Tab navigation — Page-level tabs above the card content
